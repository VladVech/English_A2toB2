Mark is student from Germany

Listen to the audio and read the script below:

Comprehension Questions

1. Where is Mark from?




2. Where does Mark study?




3. What does Mark study?




4. When did Mark arrive in Canada?




5. What kind of job does Mark have?




6. Where does he work?




7. When does Mark usually work?




8. How many evenings a week does Mark work?




9. What skill has Mark improved?




10. What kind of projects does Mark often do?




11. What is important in these projects?




12. What does Mark do on weekends?




13. What kind of events does he attend?




14. What does Mark say about life in a new country?




15. What does Mark hope to get?




16. What is his dream?




Script

Mark is an international student from Germany who moved to Canada to study computer science at a university in Ottawa. He arrived last September and has already completed two semesters.

In addition to his studies, Mark works part-time at a local IT support company. Balancing work and school is not easy, but he manages his time carefully. He usually attends lectures during the day and works three evenings a week.

Mark says that studying in English has improved both his technical vocabulary and his confidence when speaking. He often participates in group projects, where communication and teamwork are essential.

On weekends, Mark spends time exploring the city, attending networking events, or meeting with friends from different countries.

Although life in a new country can be challenging, Mark believes the experience will help him in the future. His goal is to gain permanent residency and eventually start his own tech company in Canada.
