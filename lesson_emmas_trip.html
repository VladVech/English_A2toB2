Comprehension Questions

3. What did she like about her hotel?

Script

Emma is from Toronto. Last weekend, she traveled to Vancouver for the first time. She took a flight on Friday evening and arrived at night. Her hotel was near the ocean, and she loved the view from her window.

On Saturday morning, Emma visited Stanley Park. She walked by the seawall and took many photos. In the afternoon, she had lunch at a small restaurant and tried local seafood.

On Sunday, it rained, so Emma went to a museum and a bookstore. She bought a book about Canadian history.

Emma says Vancouver is beautiful and she wants to come back in the summer.
