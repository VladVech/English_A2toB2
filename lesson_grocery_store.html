Going to the Grocery Store

Listen to the audio and read the script below:

Comprehension Questions

1. When did Sarah go to the grocery store?




2. What did she bring to the store?




3. What did she buy?




4. How did she get home?




5. What did she do in the evening?




Script

Yesterday, Sarah went to the grocery store after work.
She needed to buy some food for the week.
She made a list at home: bread, eggs, milk, vegetables, and some fruit.

At the store, she found everything easily. The apples were fresh, and the tomatoes looked good.
She also bought some pasta and a bottle of olive oil.

After paying at the checkout, she carried her bags home.
It was a short walk, and the weather was nice.
In the evening, she made a healthy dinner and watched a movie.
