Comprehension Questions

1. 1. What did Alex do on Saturday morning?

  • He watched TV
  • He cleaned his apartment
  • He went shopping

2. 3. Where did Alex meet his friends?

  • At school
  • At a restaurant
  • At home

3. 5. What did Alex do on Sunday?

  • He went to the cinema
  • He read a book and watched a movie
  • He worked in the garden

4. 6. Who did he help on Sunday?

  • His brother
  • His younger sister
  • His mother

5. 7. How did Alex feel about the weekend?

  • Tired
  • Relaxed
  • Angry

Script

My name is Alex, and I want to tell you about my weekend.
On Saturday morning, I cleaned my apartment and did some laundry. After that, I went to the park with my dog. The weather was sunny, and many people were outside.

In the evening, I met my friends at a small restaurant. We ate pizza and talked about our week.

On Sunday, I stayed at home. I read a book and watched a movie. I also called my parents and helped my younger sister with her homework.

It was a simple weekend, but I really enjoyed it. I felt relaxed and ready for the new week.
