Anna’s Morning

Listen to the audio and read the script below:

Comprehension Questions

1. What time does Anna wake up?




2. How does she go to school?




3. What does she study?




4. Where does she have lunch?




5. What does Anna do in the afternoon?




6. How does she relax in the evening?




7. What time does she go to bed?




8. How does Anna feel about her day?




Script

Anna is a student. She lives in a small apartment in the city.
Every morning, she wakes up at 7 o’clock. She takes a quick shower and drinks a cup of coffee.
After breakfast, she takes the bus to school. It usually takes 20 minutes.

Her first class starts at 8:30. She studies English and computer science.
Anna likes her teachers and enjoys learning new things.
At 12 o’clock, she has lunch with her classmates. They eat in a small café near the school.

In the afternoon, Anna sometimes goes to the library. She reads books or works on her homework.
In the evening, she watches TV or talks to her family online.
She usually goes to bed at 10 o’clock.

Anna’s days are simple, but she feels happy and busy.
